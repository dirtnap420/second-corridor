The honest margins

What the record doesn't say (yet).

  1. The construction ramp-down after 2041 is illustrative — no cited figure describes how the workforce winds down.
  2. The talent lattice's ribbon widths are illustrative; only its structure — who feeds whom — is cited.
  3. No NYISO document publishes a full-buildout megawatt figure; the cited interconnection requests stop at Fab 2.
  4. Commerce has published no award record for the Micron funding agreements — the ledger renders the absence.
  5. QCEW counts every NAICS-3344 employer in a county, not Micron headcount — the plan series and the measured series answer different questions.
  6. OEWS publishes no semiconductor-technician wage for either corridor metro — the occupation the buildout most needs is the one the survey can't yet see here.
